Sushi

A traditional Japanese dish, sushi consists of vinegared rice combined with a variety of ingredients such as fresh seafood, vegetables, and sometimes tropical fruits. It can be served in different forms, like nigiri (slices of fish atop rice), maki (rolled sushi with nori seaweed), or sashimi (thinly sliced raw fish).

Spaghetti

A classic Italian pasta dish featuring long, thin noodles made from durum wheat. Typically served with a variety of sauces, like rich marinara or creamy carbonara, and often topped with Parmesan cheese and fresh herbs.

Ramen

A beloved Japanese noodle soup, ramen consists of wheat noodles served in a savory broth, typically made from pork, chicken, or vegetables. It’s garnished with toppings like soft-boiled eggs, sliced pork, seaweed, and scallions, offering a hearty and satisfying meal.

price $200

Pounded Yam

A staple of Nigerian cuisine, pounded yam is a smooth, dough-like dish made by boiling and pounding yam until it reaches a stretchy consistency. It is often served with rich, flavorful soups or stews, such as egusi or okra soup.

price $200

Jollof Rice

A popular West African dish, especially in Nigeria, Jollof rice is a flavorful, one-pot dish cooked with tomatoes, onions, bell peppers, and spices. Often accompanied by meat or fish, it's a vibrant, spicy dish with a deep, smoky flavor.

price $200

JollofmSpaghetti

A fusion dish combining the rich, bold flavors of West African Jollof rice with Italian spaghetti. Jollof spaghetti is prepared by cooking spaghetti in a spicy, tomato-based sauce that includes onions, bell peppers, garlic, and a blend of traditional spices like thyme and bay leaves.

About us

Estiatorio

Join us for a dining experience that’s not just about food—it’s about celebrating the diverse flavors of the world.
